## Employment/timesheets

Your Digital Scholarship position will appear under the “supervisory organization” Educational and Scholarly Technology_STU  (Alice McGrath). If you have multiple jobs, you will have to select this one from the drop-down menu in order to enter time. 
Workday instructions and how to guides: 
Entering time worked: https://www.brynmawr.edu/sites/default/files/media/documents/2025-11/EnterTimeWorked.pdf 
Revising time worked: https://www.brynmawr.edu/sites/default/files/media/documents/2025-11/ReviseTimeWorked.pdf 
You may also refer to this instructional video for more information.
